Solar Run

Over the last summer I worked as the Lead Game Developer for the Presidents Global Leadership Award Singapore branch here at UT. They were researching how to spread awareness about UV Exposure and Melanoma to the citizens of Singapore. Their solution was to create a mobile browser game and an AR filter which depicts the effects of skin cancer on your face. I built the mobile browser game for them using JavaScript, Phaser.IO, PHP, and a MySQL server.

You can play it at the link below! Be warned it is designed to only be played on mobile browser, specifically iphone safari.

Bevo's Tunes
